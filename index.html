Minecraft is a great sandbox game that allows you to do anything you can think of.

The creative and building aspects of Minecraft allow players to build constructions out of textured cubes in a 3D generated world. Other activities in the game include exploration, gathering resources, crafting, and combat.

Gameplay has two principal modes: survival, which requires players to acquire resources and maintain their health and hunger; and creative, where players have an unlimited supply of resources, the ability to fly, and no health or hunger.

This game is a giant game of building. Like legos but on the computer. There are many things to do in this immersive world besides building with blocks. You can have companions, play Online with others, destroy the world, rebuild it, and shape everything into your image.
